SBIRT Wyoming: an Overview

Speaker: Rodger McDaniel, Wyoming Department of Health Deputy Director, Mental Health and Substance Abuse Services Division; Kathy Cordell, SBIRT Training Specialist; Lori Hicks, Social Services Specialist for Natrona County Health Department

Track: SBIRT

Developed and implemented under the auspices of the World Health Organization by an international group of addiction researchers, SBIRT (screening, brief intervention, referral and treatment) is a protocol designed to take advantage of the receptiveness of individuals in a doctor's office to information about how their substance use impacts their health. In this session, learn why the Wyoming Department of Health has chosen to use the Alcohol, Smoking and Substance Involvement Screening Test (ASSIST) and how you can be compensated for screenings in your practice. Receive a brief review of motivational interviewing, witness an interview, and hear the impact of SBIRT in the lives of Wyoming citizens through the eyes of those administering it.
